Our Story

We are a 15 year old professional photography services company, specializing in Wedding, Hindu Wedding, Christian Wedding, North Indian Wedding, South Indian Wedding, Indian Wedding, Bridal Portraits, Couple Portraits, Pre-wedding Shoots, Engagement and Reception photography. We offer products and services such as Shortfilms, Lamination, Coffee Table Book, Blueray Disc, Brochure, Documentary, Live Streaming, T-Shirt, Montage, Enlargement, Calendar, HD Videos, Coffee Mug, Promo Videos, Cinematography, HD Highlights, Poster, CD / DVD, Frames and Photobook. We accept customized photography requirements. Our studio is in Coimbatore. We cover events in Tamil Nadu and South India. We are comfortable communicating in Kannada, Tamil, Hindi and English.
